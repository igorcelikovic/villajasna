The house & its place in Njivice

The apartment sits on the first floor of our house, surrounded by a green, shaded garden in the quiet seaside village of Njivice. We live here all year round, so the house is always cared for — and someone is always around if you need anything.

Njivice is one of the calmest, greenest villages on Krk — Croatia's largest island, connected to the mainland by bridge and reachable from Rijeka airport in under 30 minutes. The Kvarner coast here is rocky and crystal-clear, the summers long and warm, and the whole island small enough to explore in a long weekend.

  • The beach is just steps from the garden gate
  • A short walk to Njivice's promenade, restaurants and shops
  • Easy to reach by car — minutes from the Krk bridge
